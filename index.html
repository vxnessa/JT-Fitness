Fitness Programs

Muscle Toner.

The muscle toning program will be for those who are satisfied with the weight they are at. Yet trying to scultp their bodies to reveal more defined muscles. Consisting of circuit training that will have your muscles burning.

Strength.

The strength program will focus on increasing muscle mass and improving your 1RM (1 Repition Max). This is great for people who are looking to get stronger, with most of the session focusing on weight training. The programming will be similar to power lifitng where I will insctruct how to perform heavy weight lifting safely.

Athletics.

The athletic program is a combination of both strength and agility work. We will focus on increasing your 1RM while getting quicker and faster. This will involve the first half of sessions involving weight training, and finishing off with exercises proven to make you quicker. This program can be for college athletes or if your children are in sports and you want them to stand out from their peers.

Weight Loss.

The weight loss program will help those who want to cut off a few pounds, boosting your confidence , helping you keep up with your children, and course make you feel great. This program will be focused on keeping your heart rate up, burning as many calories per session as we can.
